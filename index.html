About Me

My name is Spencer Smith and I'm the world's biggest baseball fan

Born and raised in Euless, Texas, I could never dream of calling another state my home. I graduated with my bachelors in music education from the University of Houston in 2015, spending the first two years of my career as an assistant band director at Klein Oak High School in Klein ISD. Looking to move a little closer to home, I later pursued a job as an assistant band director in Prosper ISD where I have taught since 2017.

My Skills

JavaScript

I started learning JavaScript in March of 2020 in order to pick up some web development skills in light of the Covid-19 pandemic.

HTML/CSS

Travel

My hope is that my life will have ultimately been better measured by my experiences than my possessions. In 2018 I flew solo to Africa to climb Mount Kilimanjaro, and by 2027 I plan to have attempted to climb both Denali and Aconcagua.

Baseball

At one week old, my father took me to my first Texas Rangers game and I never looked back. My interest was only compounded by the introduction of StatCast in 2015, which led to a strong desire for deeper analytical understanding.

Music

Music was my first love. I spent hours of my childhood in my garage hammering out riffs to my favorite classic rock songs and fell in love with band class early on in school. I spent the first five years of my career as a band director in Texas.
